Our Story

35 Years of Industry Experience, One Focused Mission

Smith Technology Group was built on a single conviction: enterprises deserve a technology partner who has actually lived the challenges they face — not just read about them. Our founder spent over three decades in the enterprise IT trenches, managing infrastructure, sourcing mission-critical hardware, and navigating complex procurement cycles for organizations that simply could not afford downtime.

That depth of expertise shapes everything we do. From day one, we set out to build a firm that combines the agility and personal attention of a specialized boutique with the OEM access and procurement power of a major provider.

Today, Smith Technology Group serves clients across a wide range of industries — delivering everything from compute and networking hardware to cloud strategy and 24/7 managed support.
