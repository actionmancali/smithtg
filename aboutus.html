Our Story

A Quarter Century of IT Leadership

Founded in 1999, Smith Technology Group was born from a simple belief: enterprises deserve a technology partner who thinks like they do — strategically, efficiently, and with an eye on the long game.

From our roots in hardware procurement, we've grown into a full-service infrastructure and management company. Today we serve clients across a wide range of industries, delivering everything from compute and networking to cloud migration and 24/7 managed support.

Our team brings decades of hands-on experience with the platforms your business depends on — backed by direct relationships with HP, Dell, Lenovo, and other tier-1 OEMs that translate into real advantages for our clients.
